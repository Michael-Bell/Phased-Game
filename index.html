How to Play

Up+Down+Left+Right to move

Q: Shoot a normal bullet

W: Shoot a Homing Missile

E: Bombs Away

R: Fire a lazer

P: Pause the game

S: Enable/Disable Sound Effects

M: Enable Disable background music
